About Us

MG Capital Maintenance is a commercial cleaning company servicing over 16 million square feet of Class A buildings in the Research Triangle.

Leveraging years of leadership and training experiece, we are committed to building long-term value by providing clean and safe working environments for the community.

We hope to build reliability and trust through our commitment and integrity, and become part of the the team that that makes each property unique.
